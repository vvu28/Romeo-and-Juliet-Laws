Is the Age Gap Legal?

Each state has different laws regarding legal age gaps in sexual relationships.
Most states have "Romeo and Juliet Laws" that exempt small age differences. I find this name funny.
Determine if the age gap is legal!




Laws Reference:

  • The relationship must be consensual.
  • Neither person can be a registered sex offender or in a position of authority over the other (such as a teacher, coach, or guardian).
  • The age of consent varies by state
  • Close-in-age exemptions (Romeo and Juliet Laws) vary by state.
  • Mininum age for exemption varies by state.
